8. GARDEN LEAVE: Upon termination or resignation, the Employer may place the Employee on garden leave for the notice period, during which the Employee will be paid but cannot access the workplace or company systems. Garden leave may be extended at the Employer's discretion indefinitely.

9. RESTRICTIVE COVENANTS: The Employee agrees not to solicit or deal with any clients, customers, suppliers, or employees of the Employer for 5 years after termination.

10. DISCIPLINARY ACTION: The Employer may suspend the Employee without pay for any alleged misconduct pending investigation. The investigation timeline shall be at the Employer's sole discretion.

11. CONFIDENTIALITY: The Employee shall keep confidential all information about the Employer's business. This clause survives termination and continues indefinitely.
